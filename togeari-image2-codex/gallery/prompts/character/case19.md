## Prompt

Use the reference image as the primary character identity basis. Maintain the same adult female in her 20s including facial features, expression, hairstyle, body proportions, clothing design, and overall vibe. Create a brand-new high-quality realistic photo based on the same person. Only change the camera angle and composition according to angle instructions. Do not redesign the character, do not change her age feeling, do not make her look like a different person, do not change her body type or clothing structure. Maintain a simple natural standing pose. Use a clean bright studio environment with visible floor and wall, natural realistic lighting, true perspective, and premium commercial photography quality. Avoid: extra limbs, missing fingers, hand deformation, missing body parts, face ghosting, clothing distortion, anatomical confusion.
