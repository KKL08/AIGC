# Four-Panel Retro Kitchen Scene Design

**Author:** @hanana
**Category:** illustration
**Original Case:** 4

## Prompt

Create a colorful four-panel scene-design concept sheet for a cozy retro kitchen, in a whimsical flat 2D illustration style inspired by 1980s/1990s animation background art. Wide horizontal image, 16:9 aspect ratio, divided into exactly 4 rectangular panels by thin white gutters: two panels across the top row and two panels across the bottom row. Setting: bright, playful retro kitchen with emerald green cabinets, pink trim, peach countertops, leafy trees visible outside windows, warm pastel palette. Clean ink lines, slightly imperfect hand-drawn perspective, screen-print-like colors. Panel 1 top left: wide eye-level kitchen view with green cabinets, black refrigerator with magnets, pink-and-white checkered towel, two tall windows, peach counter, pendant lighting, central peach table with donuts plate, newspaper, pink bottle, two mismatched chairs. Panel 2 top right: upward-looking corner view emphasizing ceiling, pendant lights, upper cabinets, tall windows. Panel 3 bottom left: close-up tabletop still life with donut plate, newspapers, pink bottles. Panel 4 bottom right: high overhead floor-plan view showing black-and-white checkerboard floor, green cabinetry, central table. Saturated candy colors, retro anime background feel, crisp outlines, no photorealism. No people, no pets, no speech bubbles.
