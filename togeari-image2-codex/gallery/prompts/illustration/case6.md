# Endless Megastructure Free Fall

**Author:** @おやぎ
**Category:** illustration
**Original Case:** 6

## Prompt

Create a vertical 2:3 ultra-detailed anime illustration: one young person, a faceless windblown youth, falling backward through an immense infinite architectural construction site, seen from dramatic high-angle fisheye perspective looking down into a dizzying vertical abyss. Character centered slightly above middle, body foreshortened, arms reaching upward, long dark hair flying, wearing loose off-white work jumpsuit with rolled sleeves, green safety harness straps, chunky gray-white sneakers with red accents. Surround with impossibly dense lattice of scaffolding, cables, wireframe beams, catwalks, cranes, towers, glass tubes, capsules, spherical observation pods, circular platforms, elevators, pipes, unfinished building modules, all receding endlessly downward. At least five prominent construction cranes in red and yellow. Many tiny silhouetted workers on distant catwalks for scale. Mix white concrete megablocks, transparent blue glass cylinders, yellow capsule bridges, red structural frames, green walkways. Highly intricate Japanese sci-fi illustration, clean ink lines, watercolor and marker-like color, obsessive architectural detail, bright daylight, pale cream background, cinematic vertigo.
