# Floating Sky Night Market

**Author:** @MrsSpitFire252
**Category:** illustration
**Original Case:** 5

## Prompt

Create a richly detailed fantasy concept art scene of a floating night market above the clouds, blending steampunk airships, sky docks, and colorful bazaar tents. Vertical 2:3 composition, cinematic wide-angle view. Dark navy stormy sky overhead, pale glowing horizon. Exactly 3 visible airships: 1 large oval zeppelin on upper left with dark wooden gondola, 1 smaller distant airship centered in background, 1 very large balloon-and-gondola airship docked on upper right. Exactly 5 main market areas with different colored canopies. Wooden floating piers suspended in sky by fantasy engineering, partly hidden by clouds. Stalls sell spices, fruits, fabrics, trinkets. Draped cloth awnings in deep blue, teal, red, orange, purple, and gold. Numerous warm hanging lanterns. Dense crowd of tiny merchants and shoppers. Epic fantasy illustration, atmospheric lighting, ornate steampunk design, warm golden lantern glow, cool blue shadows, volumetric clouds, cinematic depth, painterly brushwork.
