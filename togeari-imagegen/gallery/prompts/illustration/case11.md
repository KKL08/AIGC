# Two-Panel Manga Office Comic

**Author:** @魔王源
**Category:** illustration
**Original Case:** 11

## Prompt

Create a black-and-white manga-style two-panel comic page set in a modern office. Vertical page, roughly 2:3 aspect ratio, thick black outer border, two stacked rectangular panels separated by thick horizontal gutter. Detailed pencil/ink line art, grayscale screentone shading, clean manga draftsmanship, realistic office perspective. Panel 1: 3 characters in an open-plan office — tall messy-haired young man in white dress shirt with rolled sleeves and loosened necktie, middle-aged office man in suit holding papers, young woman with short bob wearing blouse and cardigan with backpack. GPT Image 2 sign in upper-right corner. Panel 2: 2 characters seated at meeting desk — bob-haired woman arms crossed looking stern, messy-haired man holding a small tag above an open storage box. Desk props: keyboard, mesh pen cup, open box, paper stack, shelf with binders. Handwritten Japanese note at top-left of Panel 2. No speech bubbles, no extra captions, grayscale only, manga line art only.
